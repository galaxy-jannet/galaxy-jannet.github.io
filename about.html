About me

✰ 21 year old creative who likes to see perfectionism as an asset rather than a peace-threatening characteristic
✰ content creator since 2016; creative since 2002
✰ can't decide between being the trend spotter and the trendsetter
✰ passionate about new media, art and combining them
✰ addicted to adobe (but in a good way, I promise)
✰ munching on chocolate wafers and sour candy in between projects

My content creation journey started in 2016, when I posted my first YouTube video on my channel. I quickly started getting traction and many people were interested in my content. After a while, brands started contacting me to collaborate, and that's when I began researching more and more about advertising, being in the middle of the way from the consumer to the brand. I fell in love with the process of creating content, both for me and for brands, and I turned my passion into my daily work.

In 2023, I graduated from FSPAC, UBB with a Bachelor's in Advertising, and I am currently a MA student at the same college, studying Digital Media and Game Studies.

The main content pillars I work with at the moment are beauty, fashion, lifestyle, parenting and pharma.
