Frequently asked questions

What kind of content do you create?

I create and static content (photos, illustrations), video content (short and long-form), and AR content in the form of Instagram AR filters. I am open to creating content for any niche, but my main ones at the moment are beauty, fashion, lifestyle, entertainment and art.

I edit all of my content myself and I like to play around with visual effects. I also do basic pictures and vlogs or montages, but expect them to be very aesthetically pleasing. My materials will either be interesting by editing style, visuals, or story. I love creating a story around products and showcase them creatively. You will not find me taking a photo with a product in my hand and call it a day - that's not authentic and we're in 2024, after all.

Do you accept gifted collabs?

I do accept gifted collabs - but it depends on how busy my schedule is, what the expectations from both parts are, how well I know the brand / product, and how well it matches my image and values. So, when I do, I'm quite selective about them. 

What are your rates?

My rates start from 150 euros (160 $) for a TikTok post or for an Instagram post (photo or reel) + a few stories.
For a YouTube video, the starting price for an insertion is 150 E, while for a dedicated video it is 400 E. 

How long do the deliverables take?

It depends on their complexity, but deadlines will be discussed from the start. Fast delivery of the materials might mean a higher rate. Also, please note that any deadline changes that imply the content must be finished earlier might be charged extra.

Do you offer an invoice for your services?

Yes, I do. 

Other things you should know

If I do not know your brand already, I might ask you to send me a sample product to test for a while first, and then decide whether or not I want to associate myself with your brand. In order to keep being transparent and maintain my audience's trust, I will not promote a product I wouldn't use myself or a product that is not good. 

I'm passionate about what I do and my goal is to create a meaningful, long-term connection with both my audience and the brands I work with. My standards are very high in everything I do and half measures don't do for me. I'm not only a social media influencer, but also a freelancer in this domain and I love that both roles allow me to be creative.
